History of the International L and R series

Prior to 1950

Introduced in 1947 was the KB, the predecessor to the L series. The KB trucks had bulky shapes, only seated two, and were harder to see out of, due to the thick A-pillars and a two piece windsheild.

The L series is born in 1950 - First version

The L series is revised in 1951 - Second version

The R series is born in 1952

The S series is born in 1955

The sucessor to the S is born in 1957... the A and B series.
