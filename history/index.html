Prior to 1950

Prior to 1950, there were the KB series trucks, which were similar in style to the L's, but had a different body style. The KB trucks had two piece front windsheild, and had a two piece folding hood. In 1950, International introduced the L series, introduced then as the "comfo-cab" line of trucks. They were called "comfo-cabs" becasue of the new one piece wndsheild, the ssmaller A-pillars, the larger rear window, and the larger cab design. The early models
